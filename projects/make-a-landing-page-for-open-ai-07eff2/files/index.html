Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about the NovaMind platform.

What makes NovaMind different from other AI platforms?▼

NovaMind is built from the ground up with a focus on multimodal understanding, safety research, and developer experience. Our models are designed to be steerable, interpretable, and aligned with human intent — not just powerful, but predictable.

How does pricing work?▼

We offer usage-based pricing with transparent per-token rates. Every plan includes a free tier so you can experiment and prototype before committing. Enterprise customers get custom rate limits and dedicated support.

Can I fine-tune models on my own data?▼

Yes. NovaMind supports fine-tuning for select models through our API and web interface. Your data stays private — we never train on customer submissions unless you explicitly opt in.

What safety measures are in place?▼

Every model undergoes rigorous red-teaming, bias evaluation, and content-safety fine-tuning before release. We provide classification endpoints, usage policies, and audit logs to help you deploy responsibly.

Do you offer on-premise deployment?▼

For enterprise customers with strict data residency requirements, we offer private cloud and on-premise deployment options. Contact our sales team for details.

What programming languages do you support?▼

Our API is language-agnostic over HTTP. We provide first-party SDKs for Python, JavaScript/TypeScript, Go, Rust, and Java, with community-maintained libraries for many others.
