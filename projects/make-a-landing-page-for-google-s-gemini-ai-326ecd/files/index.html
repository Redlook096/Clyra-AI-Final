    Frequently asked questions

    What is Gemini?

    Gemini is Google's most capable and general AI model family, developed by DeepMind and Google Research. It's built to be multimodal (processing text, images, audio, video, and code), highly efficient, and responsibly deployed across Google products and for developers via the Gemini API.

    How does Gemini differ from other AI models?

    Gemini was designed natively as a multimodal model — it's not a text model with image support bolted on. It also features a massive context window (up to 1M+ tokens in some models), strong agentic tool-use capabilities, and is built with Google's safety and responsibility principles from the ground up.

    Where can I access Gemini?

    You can access Gemini through gemini.google.com, the Gemini mobile app (iOS and Android), and via the Gemini API on Google AI Studio. It's also integrated into Google products like Gmail, Docs, and more.

    Is Gemini free to use?

    Yes! Gemini has a generous free tier available at gemini.google.com and through the Gemini API. For higher usage needs, there are paid tiers (Gemini Advanced via Google One AI Premium and API pay-as-you-go pricing) with expanded rate limits and additional features.

    Can Gemini generate and debug code?

    Absolutely. Gemini excels at code generation, explanation, debugging, and porting across 20+ programming languages including Python, JavaScript, TypeScript, Go, Rust, C++, and more. It includes strong reasoning capabilities that help it handle complex coding tasks.
